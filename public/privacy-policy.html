Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Pocket Gull, we take your privacy and the security of clinical data extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Pocket Gull application.

Data Security and HIPAA Compliance

Pocket Gull operates within a Zero-Trust architecture secured by Google Cloud Identity-Aware Proxy (IAP). We do not store or transmit Protected Health Information (PHI) outside of secured, strictly audited boundaries. All data processed by our AI orchestration layers is managed under Business Associate Agreements (BAA) and rigorous enterprise security protocols.

Information Collection

We strictly collect authentication telemetry for audit logging to ensure only authorized practitioners access the system. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your identity is verified securely via Google Workspace external identities and localized Passkeys (WebAuthn).

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact your system administrator.
